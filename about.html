About Us

Our Background

Founded in 2020, we are fairly new to the market. The owners of the Gym have pushed to created a high focus on the quality of the service we provide. We strive to ensure that all your needs are catered for and we help you achieve your fitness goals. The owners, being former sportsmen and women know what is required of fitness centers in order to help you along the journey. We aim to be one of the top rated gyms nationwide within 5 years, and we hope with your support we can work together to achieve this goal.

Why Chose us

Our Gym is a brand new gym with top of the range equipment and classes at an affordable price for all ages. We offer high-spec nationwide gyms that are inclusive and comfortable for everyone. We offer flexible, monthly contracts which you can cancel at anytime if you need to without any fees. At our Gym, we also have a range of facilities such as a swimming pool, a spa, and an indoor kids play area. We are also open 24 hours of the day so you can work out whenever suits you.



Our Values

At Our Gym, we are very committed to creating an enclusive environment in which people of all ages and abilities can come to work towards their fitness goals and maintain their physical and mental wellbeing. We understand that people are going through tough times and we are here to provide that support needed. We also value you honest and open feedback on your experience at the gym.
