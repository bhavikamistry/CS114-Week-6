Coronavirus guidelines

Due to the coronavirus outbreak we have had to implement certain changes to our facilities to keep everyone safe

Check out our COVID-19 regulations below for the gym


COVID-19 guidelines:

Please note that the guidelines may change according to government advice & certain gym locations may be closed in areas with local lockdown.


  • Do not visit the gym if you've experienced any symptoms of COVID-19

  • Follow social distancing protocols at all times whilst at the gym

  • Don't drink directly from the water fountain, use a bottle

  • Wash or sanitise your hands when entering and whilst at the gym regularly

  • Wipe down and clean any kit you've used once you're done with it
