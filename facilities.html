Our Facilities

High quality equipment for a low price

Get your heart racing and blood pumping on our specialised equipment


Weights

If one of your main goals is to build more muscle then out weights section will help you to do this. We have weights ranging from from 1kg to 50kg. We also provide squat racks and benches

Cardio

If your goal is improve on your general cardio fitness the our range of cardio machines will enable you to do so. We have machines such as treadmills, stair climbers, rowing machines, cross trainers and many more!

Swimming Pool

Swimming is one of the most effective ways to exercise. Here at the gym we offer a variety of swimming lessons in our large pools which features a kids area and a deep end

Sauna

After the pool why not head to the spa and unwind. At our center you will find a sauna and steam room
