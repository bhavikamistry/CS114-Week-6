Memberships

We allow anyone 16+ to use the facilities at Our Gym

Please see the below options for signing up to use out gym





Under 18's

Our U18 membership costs £8 a month. With this you can use the weights, cardio rooms and swimming pool. Each Month you get 5 family and friend passes where you can bring anyone you like to the gym.

Student

If you are currently studying at university, why not take advantage of out student membership. This costs £12 a month and allows you to use all the facilities we provide We understand you will be going back and forth to university throughout the year so you can choose 2 gyms in different locations you want to use.

Adult

Our adult membership costs £23 a month and allows you to use all facilities we provide. There are no joining or cancellation fees so you can cancel your contract whenever you need to. We allow you to pick 2 different gym locations to use as we want to provide flexibility to your workout.

Family

if you are an active,family who love to go to the gym then why not sign up to our family membership. This allows 5 family members to all be apart fo the same contract. This costs £100 a month and you can use all facilities available as long as you are 18+.
